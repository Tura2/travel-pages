Welcome to Paris!

Paris, the capital of France, is a city of love and romance, famous for its stunning architecture, fashion, and art. It's a must-visit destination for couples and solo travelers alike.

Top Attractions:

Eiffel Tower

The Eiffel Tower is an iconic symbol of Paris and one of the most-visited paid monuments in the world. It offers breathtaking views of the city from its top deck, especially during sunset. Visitors can take the stairs or lift to reach the top.
