Welcome to Somaliland!

Somaliland, a self-declared state, is a hidden gem for nature lovers and food enthusiasts. With its warm and welcoming people, you'll feel at home in this East African country.

Top Attractions:

Daallo Mountains

The Daallo Mountains offer breathtaking scenery, with their rugged peaks and valleys. Take a hike and experience the untouched beauty of Somaliland. The mountains are also home to a variety of flora and fauna.

Coordinates: Latitude 9.95, Longitude 43.25

Berbera Beach

Berbera Beach is a tropical paradise, with its crystal-clear waters and white sandy shores. Enjoy a relaxing day of swimming, snorkeling, or simply soaking up the sun. The beach is also a great spot for seafood lovers, with its fresh catches of the day.

Coordinates: Latitude 10.43, Longitude 45.03

Laas Geel

Laas Geel is a stunning rock art site, featuring ancient paintings and carvings. The site dates back thousands of years, and is a testament to the rich cultural heritage of Somaliland. Be amazed by the vibrant colors and intricate designs of the rock art.

Coordinates: Latitude 9.78, Longitude 43.45

Come and experience the natural beauty, rich culture, and warm hospitality of Somaliland for yourself.
