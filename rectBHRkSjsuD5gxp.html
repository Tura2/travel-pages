Welcome!

California, the Golden State, is a paradise for travelers. From the sun-kissed beaches to the snow-capped mountains, California has something for everyone. Get ready to explore the best of the West Coast!

Top Attractions:

Yosemite National Park

Yosemite National Park is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of the most beautiful national parks in the US. The park is famous for its granite cliffs, waterfalls, and Giant Sequoia groves. Hike to Yosemite Valley, visit Half Dome, or simply take in the breathtaking views.

Coordinates: Latitude 37.7749, Longitude -119.4024

Santa Monica Pier

Santa Monica Pier is a classic California landmark and a must-visit attraction in Los Angeles. This historic pier is famous for its amusement park, arcade games, and stunning ocean views. Take a stroll along the beach, grab some fish and chips, or simply enjoy the lively atmosphere.

Coordinates: Latitude 34.0104, Longitude 118.4963

Golden Gate Bridge

The Golden Gate Bridge is an iconic symbol of San Francisco and one of the most photographed landmarks in the world. Take a walk or bike ride across the bridge, offering breathtaking views of the San Francisco Bay and the city skyline.

Coordinates: Latitude 37.7749, Longitude -122.4194
