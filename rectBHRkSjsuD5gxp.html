Welcome!

California, the Golden State, is a paradise for beach lovers and adventure seekers alike. From sun-kissed beaches to scenic coastal routes, California has it all.

Top Attractions:

Venice Beach

Venice Beach is a vibrant beachside neighborhood in Los Angeles. Known for its boardwalk, street performers, and eclectic shops, it's a popular spot for people-watching and soaking up the laid-back California vibe. Don't miss the iconic Muscle Beach Gym.

Coordinates: Latitude 33.9863, Longitude -118.4647

Big Sur

Big Sur is a rugged stretch of California's Central Coast, known for its dramatic cliffs, isolated beaches, and scenic hiking trails. Take a drive along Highway 1, and stop at Bixby Bridge for breathtaking views.

Coordinates: Latitude 36.2708, Longitude -121.8122

Santa Monica Pier

Santa Monica Pier is a classic California landmark, featuring an amusement park, arcade games, and stunning ocean views. Ride the historic Ferris wheel, and enjoy the lively atmosphere.

Coordinates: Latitude 34.0105, Longitude -118.4963
