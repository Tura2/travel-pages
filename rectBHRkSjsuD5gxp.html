Welcome!

Albenia, a country nestled in the heart of the Balkan Peninsula, boasts a rich history, stunning landscapes, and a blend of cultures. From ancient ruins to vibrant cities, Albenia has something for every kind of traveler. Get ready to explore this Balkan gem!

Top Attractions:

Butrint

This ancient city is a must-visit for history buffs. Wander through the ruins of the Roman amphitheater, explore the baptistery, and marvel at the stunning mosaics. With its rich history and breathtaking scenery, Butrint is an unforgettable experience.

Coordinates: Latitude 39.7442, Longitude 20.0225

Blue Eye

This natural wonder is a sight to behold. The Blue Eye, a mesmerizing spring, bubbles up from the earth, creating a dazzling display of turquoise water. Take a boat ride or simply sit back and enjoy the tranquility of this breathtaking spot.

Coordinates: Latitude 39.9333, Longitude 20.0333

Berat

This charming town is a treasure trove of Ottoman architecture, with its picturesque streets, historic mosques, and quaint shops. Take a stroll along the cobblestone streets and soak up the atmosphere, or visit the stunning Berat Castle.

Coordinates: Latitude null, Longitude null
