Welcome!

Paris, the capital of France, is a city like no other. Known as the City of Light, it is a treasure trove of art, fashion, and romance. From iconic landmarks to charming streets of Montmartre, there's something for everyone.

Top Attractions:

Eiffel Tower

The Eiffel Tower is an iconic symbol of Paris and one of the most recognizable landmarks in the world. Built for the 1889 World's Fair, it stands 324 meters tall and offers breathtaking views of the city. Visitors can take the elevator or stairs to the top for an unforgettable experience.

Coordinates: Latitude 48.8583, Longitude 2.2945

Louvre Museum

The Louvre Museum is one of the world's largest and most famous museums, housing an impressive collection of art and artifacts from around the world. The museum is home to the Mona Lisa, Venus de Milo, and many other famous works of art. Visitors can easily spend days exploring the vast collections.

Coordinates: Latitude 48.8634, Longitude 2.2943

Arc de Triomphe

The Arc de Triomphe is a monumental arch that honors the soldiers who fought and died for France. It stands in the center of the famous Champs-Élysées and offers stunning views of the city. Visitors can climb to the top for a panoramic view of Paris.

Coordinates: Latitude 48.8738, Longitude 2.2947
