Welcome!

Albenia, a small country nestled in the heart of Europe, is a hidden gem waiting to be discovered. With its rich history, stunning landscapes, and warm hospitality, Albenia is an ideal destination for travelers seeking an unforgettable experience.

Top Attractions:

Lake Kruja

Lake Kruja, a tranquil oasis surrounded by lush green forests, offers breathtaking views and a serene atmosphere perfect for relaxation. Visitors can enjoy boat rides, fishing, or simply take a leisurely walk around the lake. The surrounding hills are also ideal for trekking and horseback riding.

Coordinates: Latitude 41.5111, Longitude 19.7944

Butrint Ancient City

The ancient city of Butrint, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, is a must-visit attraction in Albenia. With its well-preserved ruins, including a theater, baptistery, and a museum, Butrint offers a fascinating glimpse into the past. Visitors can explore the site on their own or join a guided tour.

Coordinates: Latitude 39.7442, Longitude 20.0225

Blue Eye Spring

The Blue Eye Spring, a natural wonder, is a mesmerizing sight to behold. The crystal-clear water, surrounded by lush vegetation, creates a picturesque scene. Visitors can swim in the spring or simply relax in the surrounding area.

Coordinates: Latitude null, Longitude null
