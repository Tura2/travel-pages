Yosemite National Park

Yosemite is one of the most iconic national parks in the United States. Known for its granite cliffs, waterfalls, and giant sequoia groves, Yosemite is a nature lover's dream destination. Hike to Yosemite Valley, explore the High Sierra, or simply take in the breathtaking views.
