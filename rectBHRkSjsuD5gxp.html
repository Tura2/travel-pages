Welcome!

California, the Golden State, is a paradise for travelers. From the sun-kissed beaches to the snow-capped mountains, California has something for everyone. Get ready to explore the best of the West Coast!

Top Attractions:

Yosemite National Park

Yosemite National Park is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of the most beautiful national parks in the US. The park is famous for its granite cliffs, waterfalls, and giant sequoia groves. Hike to Yosemite Valley, visit Half Dome, or explore the High Sierra.

Coordinates: Latitude 37.7749, Longitude -119.4024

Santa Monica Pier

The Santa Monica Pier is a classic California landmark and a must-visit attraction. This historic pier is home to the famous Pacific Park amusement park, an aquarium, and plenty of restaurants and shops. Take a stroll along the beach, ride the Ferris wheel, or simply enjoy the sunset.

Coordinates: Latitude 34.0104, Longitude -118.4963

Golden Gate Bridge

The Golden Gate Bridge is an iconic symbol of San Francisco and one of the most photographed bridges in the world. Take a walk or bike ride across the bridge, visit the Golden Gate Bridge Pavilion, or simply take in the stunning views of the San Francisco Bay.

Coordinates: Latitude 37.7749, Longitude -122.4194
