Catedral de Santiago de Compostela

The Cathedral of Santiago de Compostela is a beautiful Gothic cathedral and a significant pilgrimage site. Located in the northwest region of Spain, this stunning cathedral is said to be the final resting place of Saint James the Greater. Its intricate stone carvings, stunning stained-glass windows, and peaceful atmosphere make it a truly spiritual experience.
